Signature Experiences

Beyond the game drives, five hand-picked experiences have been woven through the programme — each one designed to connect your team, surprise and delight, and create the kind of shared memories that define a group forever.

Safari Walk

Greater Mara

An exclusive guided walk through Greater Mara, led by armed Maasai rangers. Encounter the Mara ecosystem at ground level — tracks, plants and wildlife at a pace no game drive can match.

Yoga on the Plains

Morning session

A sunrise yoga session on the open plains led by a visiting instructor, with the Mara's boundless horizon as your studio. A moment of stillness before the day unfolds.

Sound Healing

Dawn at camp

A guided sound bath before the savanna fully wakes, using crystal singing bowls and resonant tones. A deeply restorative start to the day — unlike anything a conference agenda has ever offered.

Tribal Dance & Bush Gala

Gala evening

A Bush Gala Dinner set in the open wilderness, lit by lanterns and firelight. Maasai warriors perform traditional tribal dances as night falls over the Mara — an evening nobody will forget.

Paint & Sip

Sundowner

Guests capture the Mara landscape on canvas with guided watercolours as the plains turn amber at dusk. No artistic experience required — only a sense of wonder.

Private Safari Fleet

A group of 47 guests travels in a dedicated fleet of private 4×4 safari vehicles, each with its own licensed driver-guide. The open-roof vehicles allow unobstructed 360° wildlife viewing, and the fleet operates on a shared radio network so all vehicles converge on key sightings simultaneously. The group dines, sleeps and experiences activities together — but each vehicle moves with its own rhythm on the plains.
